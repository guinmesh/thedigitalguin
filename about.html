Hi! I'm Guin. I'm a product designer based in Brooklyn, NY.

By engaging with empathic and facilitative tactics, I help brands, institutions, and organizations revolutionize their digital experiences.

In 2023, I completed an MFA in Design and Technology at the Parsons School of Design, where I explored the cross-section of technology, graphic design, botany, built environments, and fine art.

Ethos

I approach design with curiosity, first and foremost. As a nerd, community member, and perpetual optimist, I try to let the small joys of life bleed into my work. Excitement, wonder, and personal connection make all tasks more interesting and enjoyable.

The physical world and our interior worlds are always in conversation. The better these connect and reciprocate, the more mutual understanding, engagement, and interaction we find.
