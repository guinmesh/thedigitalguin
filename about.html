About

Guin (she/they) is a visual designer, interaction designer, and technical writer based in New York, NY. She is currently pursuing an MFA in Design and Technology at the Parsons School of Design, where she explores the cross-section of technology, graphic design, botany, built environments, and fine art. Guin is currently working on several projects in installation, web design, and general graphic design.

She was most recently a Technical Writer at Qualtrics and a Climate Science Research Assistant at the Parsons School of Design. Guin has extensive experience in visual design, technical communication, strategy, and collaboration, and she has a passion for connecting people to nature.

Guin graduated with a BFA in Studio Art and Biology from Swarthmore College '19, where she staged a thesis exhibition in painting, worked as a Graphic Designer for the Center for Innovation and Leadership, and captained the varsity volleyball team.
